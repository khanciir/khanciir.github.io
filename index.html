I am a recent BSc (Hons) graduate from Heriot-Watt University with a strong interest in human cognition and its intersections with other domains. I am currently preparing MSc applications in integrative neuroscience, where I aim to further explore the mechanisms underlying brain-behavior relationships using data-driven methods.

My research experience spans cognitive, social, and educational psychology. I have independently developed proficiency in R and Python, and I am currently expanding my skills with MATLAB.

As I prepare for postgraduate study, I am eager to contribute to neuroscience and cognition-focused projects. I am particularly interested in gaining hands-on experience with computational modelling and neuroimaging. I am also open to interdisciplinary research that applies these methods across neuroscience, cognitive science, or technology to explore novel questions about the mind and brain.
